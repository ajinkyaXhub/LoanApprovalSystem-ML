🧠 Meaning of Each Input (Simple + Exam Ready)
1. Dependents
Number of people financially dependent on applicant
👉 Example:

0 = no dependents
2 = spouse + child


2. Education
Education level of applicant
Graduate
Not Graduate

👉 Used to judge earning potential



3. Self Employed
Whether person works independently
Yes = business / freelancer
No = salaried

👉 Banks see self-employed as slightly risky



4. Annual Income
Total yearly income of applicant
👉 Example:

500000 = 5 lakh
1000000 = 10 lakh



5. Loan Amount
Amount user wants to borrow
👉 Example:

1000000 = 10 lakh loan



6. Loan Term
Duration to repay loan (in months or years depending dataset)
👉 Example:

12 = 1 year
24 = 2 years



7. CIBIL Score 
Credit score (300–900)
👉 Meaning:

750+ → very good
650–750 → average
<650 → risky



8. Residential Assets
Value of house/property owned
👉 Example:

5000000 = 50 lakh house


9. Commercial Assets
Value of business property (shops, offices)


10. Luxury Assets
Cars, gold, expensive items


11. Bank Assets
Money in bank accounts
